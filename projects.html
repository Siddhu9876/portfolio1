StaySelect (Startup Project)

StaySelect is a hostel management platform designed to help users easily find, book, and manage hostel accommodations. Aimed at students, solo travelers, and budget-conscious individuals, the platform provides a seamless experience for discovering affordable lodging. Key features include:

1. Advanced Search & Filter: Search hostels by location, price, amenities, and ratings, with personalized recommendations.

2. Interactive Map & Location-Based Suggestions: View hostel locations on an integrated map (Leaflet.js) and receive nearby attraction and transport suggestions.

3. User Reviews & Ratings: Read and submit reviews, helping users make informed decisions based on real experiences.

4. Secure Booking & Multiple Payment Options: Hassle-free, secure reservation system with support for various payment methods (credit cards, online wallets).

5. Responsive & Mobile-Friendly UI: Enjoy a smooth, accessible experience on any device, with auser-centric design.

6. Hostel Management Dashboard: Hostel owners can manage listings, availability, and bookings in real-time via a dedicated dashboard.

7. Booking History & Profile Management: Users can view past bookings and manage profiles for easier future reservations.

8. Admin Dashboard & 24/7 Customer Support: Admins can monitor platform activity, while customer support is available around the clock for assistance.

9. Booking Customization & Notifications: Customize bookings with special requests, room preferences, and receive instant booking confirmations and updates.

10. Social Media Integration & Language Support: Share bookings or reviews on social media and access the platform in multiple languages to cater to global users.

HTML, CSS, JS, Python, SQLite, Leaflet.js
